Gautam Gambhir (born 14 October 1981) is an Indian former international cricketer, former politician, a philanthropist, and the current head coach of the Indian cricket team in all formats. He played for India in all formats of the game between 2003 and 2016, and was considered as one of the greatest openers in his generation.
